Goals

CossiePlay is a company that has been operating within Hulkstown for 4 years. They specialise in custom-made costumes with advanced features. This website presents information about the company, its history, information about Cosplay, some party guidelines and specials, contact information and links.

 

Success Evaluation

Success of this site will be based on the amount of "hits" (count of people who visit the site), as well as how many new customers and enquiries CossiePlay receives after the site goes live.

Target Audience

The target audience for this site would be early teens, 13-15 years to adults, 30-40 years old. The site will be designed to cater for this target audience by the use of the photos effectively throughout the site, information that keeps the target audience intrigued and relates to the target audience, as well as a newly designed logo and neutral colours.
